About Gluten Intolerance

Gluten is a protein found in wheat, barley, and rye. Some people have difficulty digesting gluten, which can cause symptoms like bloating, stomach pain, and fatigue.

Celiac disease is an autoimmune disorder, while non-celiac gluten sensitivity causes symptoms without intestinal damage.

My Experiment

I extracted gluten from flour, treated it with plant extracts, and measured breakdown using protein test strips.

Results & Observations

  • Best breakdown: Pineapple + Ginger + Aloe
  • Pineapple and papaya strongest individually
  • Ginger alone showed little breakdown
